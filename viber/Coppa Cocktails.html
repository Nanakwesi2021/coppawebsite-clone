Nope! No fridge required. 🙌
The Bag-in-Box system keeps the cocktail sealed inside an airtight inner bag, protecting it from air and helping it stay fresh at room temperature. Just store it in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight.
That said, we always recommend chilling before serving — cold cocktails are happy cocktails. ❄️🍸
Good news: it’s about as simple as it gets. 😌
Bag-in-Box formats are designed for easy, low-tech dispensing. You can serve using the built-in tap system - no gas, no pressure, no complicated setup.
Open, pour, serve. Bar-quality cocktails without the drama. 🍸✨
Think flexible, efficient and fast. 🚀
Bag-in-Box is ideal for high-turnover venues and events like bars, pop-ups, catering services, weddings, private parties or corporate events — especially where space is limited and speed matters. Less prep, less waste, smoother service.
As for serves (based on a recommended 150 ml per cocktail):
· A 10 L Bag-in-Box delivers around 65–66 cocktails
That’s a lot of great drinks from one box — with zero shaking and minimal stress. 😎🍸
Easy and eco-friendly. ♻️
Once empty, simply separate the cardboard outer box from the inner plastic bag. The box goes into paper recycling, and the bag can be recycled according to local plastic recycling guidelines.
Less waste, more good vibes. 🌍✨
